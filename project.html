One of my favorite places in the world has to be paris for the Eiffel tower.

The Eiffel Tower is a world-famous landmark in Paris, France, standing at 330 meters (1,083 feet) tall.

Designed by Gustave Eiffel, it was built for the 1889 World's Fair to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution.

Initially criticized, it later became a global symbol of France and a major tourist attraction.

Made of wrought iron, it weighs about 10,100 tons and was the tallest structure in the world until 1930.

Today, it attracts millions of visitors annually, offering stunning views of Paris from its three observation decks.
